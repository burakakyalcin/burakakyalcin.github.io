2. Subscriptions & refunds

Sajdah Premium is purchased through the Apple App Store. Subscriptions are managed by Apple.

  • Cancel / manage: iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Sajdah
  • Refunds: handled according to Apple’s refund policy and process.
  • If you believe you were charged incorrectly, email support with your receipt details (if available).

3. Prayer times accuracy

Prayer times can vary based on calculation method, madhhab/parameters, location, and device time settings.

  • Check your selected calculation method and any regional settings.
  • Verify your device date/time and time zone are correct.
  • If you see a consistent offset, share your city, method, and the expected times so we can review.

4. iCloud sync

Sajdah can sync via Apple iCloud/CloudKit when enabled.

  • Enable in iOS: Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → toggle Sajdah (if shown)
  • Make sure you’re signed into the same Apple ID on both devices.
  • Sync may take some time; keep the app opened briefly on both devices.

Because sync is provided by Apple, availability may depend on iCloud status and connectivity.

5. Notifications

Sajdah uses local notifications scheduled on your device.

  • Check permission: iOS Settings → Notifications → Sajdah → allow notifications
  • Check Focus modes (Do Not Disturb) and notification summaries.
  • Low Power mode and background restrictions can affect timely delivery.
  • If you changed prayer time settings, open the app once to refresh schedules.
