What Sajdah is

Sajdah is a clean, lightweight prayer companion designed for daily use: prayer times, reminders, qibla, and simple tracking—without accounts.

Data stays on-device. If you enable iCloud, Apple iCloud/CloudKit can sync your data across your devices. Sajdah does not store your app data on its own servers.

No account No GPS Local notifications iCloud sync (optional) Basic anonymous analytics (TelemetryDeck)

Core features

  • Prayer times & upcoming prayer
  • Local reminders
  • Qibla direction
  • Prayer logging & consistency
  • Insights & streaks

Content

  • 99 Names of Allah (Asma-ul-Husna)
  • Daily Hadith & Islamic content
  • Sources: verified resources online and books
