Privacy Policy for Neap

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

At Neap ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our focus and productivity application.

Information We Collect

App Usage Data

We collect minimal analytics data to improve the app experience, including:

  • App open and background events
  • Session duration and completion rates
  • Feature usage patterns (settings accessed, buttons clicked)
  • Widget interactions

This data is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Screen Time Data

Neap uses Apple's Screen Time API to block selected apps during focus sessions. This data:

  • Remains entirely on your device
  • Is never transmitted to our servers
  • Is managed entirely by iOS system frameworks
  • Can only be accessed with your explicit permission

Purchase Information

Premium subscriptions are handled through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat. We receive:

  • Subscription status (active/inactive)
  • Subscription type (weekly/yearly)
  • Anonymous customer identifier

We do not receive or store your payment information.

How We Use Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Understand feature usage patterns
  • Manage premium subscription access

Data Storage and Security

  • All personal settings and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect any transmitted data
  • We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For managing premium subscriptions (see their privacy policy at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)
  • Apple Screen Time API: For app blocking functionality (managed entirely by iOS)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Deny or revoke Screen Time permissions at any time in iOS Settings
  • Delete the app and all associated local data
  • Cancel your subscription through the App Store
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Neap is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
