Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a focus session?
Open Neap and select your desired duration (1, 2, 4, or 8 minutes for free users; custom durations for premium users). Choose your commitment mode (Normal or Hard Mode) and tap "Start Session". Your selected apps will be blocked for the duration.
What's the difference between Normal and Hard Mode?
Normal Mode: You can end your session early if needed by tapping "End Session".
Hard Mode: Once started, the session cannot be ended early. Apps remain blocked until the timer completes. This mode helps build stronger focus habits.
Why can't I block certain apps?
Some system apps and essential services cannot be blocked due to iOS restrictions. Neap can only block apps that Apple's Screen Time API allows. Make sure you've granted all necessary permissions in Settings > Screen Time.
How do I select apps to block?
Go to Settings within Neap and tap "Select Apps to Block". You can choose individual apps or entire categories (like Social Networking or Games). Your selections are saved for all future sessions.
What are the premium features?
Premium subscription unlocks:
• Custom session durations (up to 24 hours)
• Widget access for quick session starts
• Custom wave colors for personalization
• Priority support
• All future premium features
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions on your iPhone, find Neap, and tap "Cancel Subscription". You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
Can I use Neap on multiple devices?
Yes! Your premium subscription is tied to your Apple ID and can be used on all devices signed in with the same Apple ID. However, blocked app selections are device-specific due to Screen Time limitations.

Troubleshooting

Apps aren't being blocked

  1. Ensure Screen Time is enabled in iOS Settings
  2. Check that Neap has permission to manage Screen Time
  3. Verify you've selected apps to block in Neap's settings
  4. Restart your device if the issue persists

Widget not appearing

  1. Ensure you have an active premium subscription
  2. Long-press on your home screen and tap the + button
  3. Search for "Neap" and add the widget
  4. Make sure you've selected apps to block before using the widget

Subscription not recognized

  1. Tap "Restore Purchases" in Neap's premium section
  2. Ensure you're signed in with the correct Apple ID
  3. Check your subscription status in iOS Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions
  4. Wait a few minutes and restart the app

Privacy & Security

Neap respects your privacy. All app blocking is handled locally on your device through Apple's Screen Time API. We don't collect or store information about which apps you use or block. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
